History

Computer Science is an applied science of basic sciences that experiences very rapid
development along with the rapid development of Science and Technology. Mastery in the field of computer science lately is deemed necessary in increasing human
resources as demands of technological development. Particularly in supporting the improvement of the quality of Higher
Education Tridarma within institutions and to support the processes of community development (regional and national), the field of
computer science is deemed necessary to be developed at Udayana University (UNUD).

Spesialisasi

There are nine specialisations in the FMIPA UNUD Informatics Study Program. The goal of the specialisations are to enable students to focus
more on specific research topics. This specialisations can accelerate the process of the final project assignment of students because small
discussion groups will be formed on each group. Each track offers 6 topic specific courses which must be taken in the 6th and 7th with 4, and 2
courses respectively. Students who are in the same specialisation will take the same elective courses together. So that it will create an
atmosphere of knowledge sharing and mutual support in conducting research on topics on the same group.
